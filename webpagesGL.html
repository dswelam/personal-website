Hello! My name is Dania Swelam, I am currently a junior at NC State University. I am majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Statistics. I also plan on doing an Artificial Intelligence Concentration. I chose to major in Computer Science because it allows me to express my creativity through coding and software development. Whether it's software design, developing algorithms, or building innovative systems, the process of engineering offers me the opportunity to express my creativity while contributing to advancements that shape the world around us.

Before enrolling at NC State, I held a certification as an English as a Foreign Language Teacher. Over the span of ten years, with six of those dedicated to teaching English to children and young adults in Saudi Arabia, I found immense gratification in this role. Being able to spread knowledge as an educator filled me with joy and satisfaction. As my passion for computer science grew, I became eager to further my own learning and pursue a degree in the field. I hope to become a Full Stack Software Developer post-graduation.
